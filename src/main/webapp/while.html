while loop

While Loop is used to execute a single statement or a block of statement n times till the condition is true.
In simple words we can say that loop is a way to keep executing either a single statement or
a block of statement n times till the condition is true i.e. until a given condition becomes false.
Every loop contains three parts:
1. Initialization 2. Condition 3. Increment/Decrement
